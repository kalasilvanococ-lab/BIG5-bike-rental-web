About BIG5 Bicycle Rentals

BIG5 is a locally operated bicycle rental service in Cagayan de Oro (CDO) that provides premium bikes, transparent rates, and friendly support 24/7. We make it easy for visitors and locals to explore Cagayan de Oro and nearby trails with confidence and comfort.

Our Shop

At BIG5, we’ve built a bike fleet with mountain, road, hybrid, city, and electric models kept in excellent condition. Each rental includes preventive maintenance checks, dependable accessories, and safety equipment. Our mission is helping riders discover more itinerary options, stay safe on their ride, and enjoy the outdoors.

We offer flexible booking, real-time rental tracking, and competitive pricing from hourly to daily packages. Senior and student discounts are available upon request.
